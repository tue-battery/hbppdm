## Author: -YH 2021-2026

There are three ocp problems in this folder, which were also used in the CDC/Automatica papers.

pendulumc.m and pendulumunc.m are the constraint and loosly constraint problems with the inverted pendulum dynamics.

ATFI16func.m describes the F16 problem with tight constraints.

The difficulty ranking is as follows: (easy to hard)
1. pendulumunc.m
2. pendulumc.m
3. ATFI16func.m

I extended the OCP problems with the eco-driving cases from the IFAC WC 2026.

synthetic benchmark:
4. ecodrivingOCP.m
